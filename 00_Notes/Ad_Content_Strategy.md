## Production Pipeline

### Why the Characters Don't Speak
- AI lip sync still looks off — viewers clock it instantly and trust is gone
- The POV format doesn't need dialogue — the text does all the talking
- Silent reaction + trending audio is the native TikTok/Reels pattern — it feels more real, not less
- No voice = no accent issues, no AI voice uncanny valley, no re-recording

---

### Video Structure (Every Single Ad)

```
[0:00 – 0:01]  →  POV text slams onto screen
[0:01 – 0:04]  →  Animated reaction shot (3 seconds of pure emotion, no words)
[0:04 – 0:15]  →  Screen recording of the app doing its thing
[0:15 – 0:18]  →  End card / CTA (link in bio, app name)
```

**Total: 15–18 seconds. Do not go longer.**

---

### Step-by-Step Production Workflow

**Step 1 — Generate the image**
Use the AI Image Prompts above. Tools: Midjourney, FLUX, or ChatGPT image gen. Get a clean still first.

**Step 2 — Animate in Google Flow / Veo**
You want micro-movement only. The character should:
- Blink
- Slowly tilt their head
- Eyes shift slightly
- Maybe a slow exhale or subtle hand movement

Do **not** prompt for big gestures or walking. Static face with tiny life in it = most believable.

**Flow/Veo Animation Prompt (use this for every archetype, swap the emotion):**

> *"Subtle realistic animation. The person blinks slowly, slight head tilt, eyes shift downward then back to camera. No speaking. No mouth movement. Hold for 3 seconds. Hyper-realistic, candid UGC style."*

**Per-archetype animation guidance:**

| Archetype | What to Animate |
|---|---|
| Mind-Blown Discovery | Eyes go wide slowly, hand rises to cover mouth, slight backward lean |
| Overwhelmed to Relieved | Head drops into hands, slow exhale, looks back up at camera |
| Smug Secret Flex | Slow blink, corner of mouth curls into a smirk, eyes hold camera |
| Lean In Whisper | Body eases forward toward camera, eyebrows raise slightly |
| Finally Done Celebration | Arms slowly rise, head tilts back, exhale, then looks at camera with relief |

**Step 3 — Screen record the app**
Record on phone or PC. Key moments to capture:
- Jay typing a topic suggestion → gets approved
- Chapter 1 generating and filling the screen
- Full outline appearing (Chapters 1–5 listed)
- The ₦15,000 unlock screen
- A WhatsApp-style notification: *"Your project is ready"

Record all of these **once** as raw clips — you'll reuse them across every video.

**Step 4 — Edit in CapCut**
- Drop Flow animation clip in
- Add POV text (bold, white, black outline — native TikTok font)
- Cut to screen recording
- Add trending audio underneath (no voiceover)
- Export 1080×1920

### Audio Strategy

Don't use original audio. Pull trending sounds from TikTok matched to your hook vibe:

| Hook Type | Audio Vibe |
|---|---|
| Pain / Panic | Dramatic or suspenseful trending audio |
| Flex / Confidence | Confident, something with a popular rap snippet |
| Whisper / Secret | Lo-fi or quiet trending audio |
| Celebration | Upbeat, euphoric trending sound |

### The Reusable Demo Asset System

Record **one master demo session** — ~60 seconds of raw screen recording hitting every key moment. Slice from it for every video. You're just swapping the reaction shot and hook text each time.

| Demo Clip | Record Once | Use For |
|---|---|---|
| Jay suggesting a topic | ✅ | All realization hooks |
| Chapter 1 generating | ✅ | All panic hooks |
| Full outline (Ch 1–5) | ✅ | All flex hooks |
| Pricing screen ₦15k | ✅ | All whisper hooks |
| Download / done screen | ✅ | All celebration hooks |

## The VC Rule

Treat content like venture capital. Most videos won't pop — one will. That one pays for all the others.

- Shoot all 5 archetypes at least twice with different hooks
- Post daily for 2 weeks
- Watch which hook type your audience responds to
- Double down on what works, drop what doesn't

**You don't need 10 accounts. You need 10 videos.**
